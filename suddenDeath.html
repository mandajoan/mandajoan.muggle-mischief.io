Welcome, muggles, to your SUDDEN DEATH cHALLENGE!

The rules are quite simple and the game is quite deadly [hence, the name].

⚡ ⚯ ✨


Upon clicking start a picture will appear.
The player who clicks their respective button first
and correctly identify the subject of the picture is the winner of the game.
If you click first but answer incorrectly, you will not be able to try again
and the other player will be allowed a turn.
If the second player to answer does so correctly, they are the winner of the game.
If both players answer incorrectly, both lose the game and it ends with no winner.

Ideally both of players would place their hands in their laps after click start,
as there is a short delay to ensure no unfair advantage to the player who clicks start.
But this game is called MUGGLE MISCHIEF, so do what you want but you are responsible for
any injuries or hurt feelings.
